Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. This policy explains how ClothMatics AI collects, uses and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, profile picture (Google Sign-In), wardrobe images, outfit photos, AI analysis results, preferences, and anonymous device analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI outfit analysis
  • Generate personalized recommendations
  • Organize your wardrobe
  • Improve application performance
  • Maintain security and reliability

3. AI Processing

Images uploaded for analysis may be securely processed by trusted AI providers solely to deliver requested features. ClothMatics does not use your uploads to train public AI models.

4. Image Storage

Wardrobe images may be stored securely to support wardrobe management and history. You can delete your uploaded items from the app.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use Firebase Authentication, Firebase Firestore, Cloudflare, Google AI services and Google Play Services.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information. No internet service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

ClothMatics is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Privacy summary

ClothMatics uses your information to provide account access, maintain your digital wardrobe, generate outfit suggestions, synchronize saved looks, and protect the service. The website does not let you upload garments. Website outfit generation sends selected wardrobe metadata to the AI service; it does not send garment image files.

1. Scope and responsibility

This Privacy Policy applies to the ClothMatics mobile application, the ClothMatics website, and related services operated under the ClothMatics name (“ClothMatics,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). It does not govern third-party websites that you visit through external links.

3. How we use information

  • Authenticate your account and synchronize it between mobile and web.
  • Create, organize, search, and display your digital wardrobe.
  • Generate personalized outfits based on items you own, preferences, occasions, and relevant profile details.
  • Save outfits, favorites, Lookbook entries, laundry status, and wear history.
  • Monitor availability, diagnose failures, prevent abuse, enforce usage limits, and improve service reliability.
  • Respond to support, privacy, deletion, or legal requests.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use garment data for advertising.

4. AI processing

ClothMatics uses Google’s Gemini API and may use other configured AI providers to produce garment analysis or outfit recommendations. For website outfit generation, the server sends garment IDs and descriptive metadata—such as category, color, material, fit, season, occasion, and laundry status—plus the occasion and optional preference. The website generator does not send garment image bytes.

AI output can be inaccurate or unsuitable. Recommendations are informational styling suggestions and should be reviewed before use. Provider handling can vary by service tier and configuration; see the Gemini API terms and Gemini retention information.

5. Service providers and disclosure

We use service providers only where needed to operate ClothMatics:

  • Google Firebase: authentication, Firestore database, and storage.
  • Google Gemini API: AI-supported analysis and outfit generation.
  • Cloudflare: website hosting, server functions, delivery, security, and optional rate-limit storage.

These providers may process information in countries other than your own under their applicable terms. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect users or the service, or as part of a business transfer subject to appropriate safeguards.

6. Retention

Account, wardrobe, profile, and saved-outfit data are generally retained while your account remains active. Operational logs and AI response records may be retained for security, reliability, usage measurement, and product operation. Provider-side retention is controlled by the provider’s terms and the service tier we use.

When you request deletion, we will take reasonable steps to remove or de-identify data under our control, subject to security, fraud-prevention, backup, dispute, and legal-retention requirements.

7. Security and access

We use HTTPS, Firebase Authentication, owner-based Firestore Security Rules, server-side API credentials, and restricted administrator access. Website wardrobe access is read-only. Only the authenticated owner may query their wardrobe through the website. Authorized administration requires a Firebase custom claim and an approved administrator account.

No system is completely secure. Protect your account password and contact us if you suspect unauthorized access.

8. Your choices and rights

Depending on applicable law, you may ask to access, correct, export, or delete personal information, withdraw consent where processing relies on consent, or raise a grievance. You can update many profile and wardrobe details in the mobile app. To request account or data deletion, contact us from the email associated with your account.

We may verify your identity before completing a request. Some information may be retained where legally permitted or required.

9. Age requirements

ClothMatics and its AI-powered features are not directed to individuals under 18. If you believe a person under 18 has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when features, providers, or legal requirements change. The current version will be posted here with a revised effective date. Material changes may also be communicated through the app, website, or registered email where appropriate.

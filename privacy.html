Privacy Policy

1. Information Collection

ClothMatics collects information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, upload photos, or contact us. We also collect device information and usage data to improve our service.

2. Use of Information

We use your information to provide and improve ClothMatics, personalize your experience, and communicate with you. Your wardrobe data helps our AI provide better recommendations.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Your information is encrypted and stored securely on Firebase servers. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third Party Services

ClothMatics uses Google Firebase for authentication and storage. We comply with Google's privacy policies. Your photos are processed using Google Cloud's image analysis services.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@clothmatics.com to exercise these rights.

6. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify users of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

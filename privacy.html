Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

Welcome to ClothMatics ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how ClothMatics collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website.

By using ClothMatics, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (when signing in with Google)
  • User preferences and profile settings

Wardrobe Information

  • Clothing images
  • Categories
  • Colors
  • Brand (if provided)
  • Descriptions and tags

Outfit Analysis

  • Uploaded outfit photos
  • AI analysis results
  • Style scores
  • Recommendations

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • Application version
  • Crash logs
  • Anonymous usage analytics

We do not collect passwords, payment information, government identification, or other highly sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide AI-powered outfit analysis.
  • Create personalized outfit recommendations.
  • Manage your digital wardrobe.
  • Improve recommendation quality.
  • Improve application performance.
  • Detect and resolve technical issues.
  • Maintain account security.

3. AI Processing

ClothMatics uses artificial intelligence to analyze clothing images and generate personalized style recommendations.

Uploaded images may be securely processed by trusted AI service providers solely to provide the requested features. Images are not used by ClothMatics to train public AI models.

4. Image Storage

Images uploaded by users may be securely stored in cloud storage to support wardrobe management and outfit history.

Users can remove their uploaded clothing items at any time. Deleted content will be removed within a reasonable period, subject to backup retention policies.

5. Authentication

If you sign in using Google, we receive only the information necessary to create your account, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture

We never receive or store your Google password.

6. Third-Party Services

ClothMatics may use trusted third-party services including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore
  • Cloudflare
  • Google AI Services
  • Google Play Services

These providers maintain their own privacy policies governing their handling of data.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

ClothMatics is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Request a copy of your information.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
